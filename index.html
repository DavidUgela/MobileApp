About

Greenwich Fitness 24/7 in Greenwich relaxed fitness studio

Greenwich Fitness has been carefully designed to help you reach your health and fitness goals. Whether you're a gym pro or completely new to exercise, you'll have access to all the latest gym equipment and over 40 free fitness classes.

Greenwich Fitness 24/7 in Greenwich relaxed fitness studio

We're based on Meridian House, Royal Hill, Greenwich, London SE10 8RD next to the Metropolitan Police. If you're coming by car, pop SE10 8RD into your SatNav and park in the pay and display bays on the road or the pay and display car park in front of the Salisbury Supermarket or in front of GSM, our Gym is inside the building. The nearest free street parking is eight minutes away on Friendly Street. The club entrance is down an open-gated side alley - look out for the Greenwich Fitness A-board. Alternatively, the 177 bus stops right outside the club, Greenwich Rail and DLR station is 3 minutes away and Deptford Bridge DLR is 3 minutes away.

Services

Club Services

At Greenwich Fitness we are dedicated to helping you achieve your fitness goals - whether you have decided you want to get in shape, train for a specific sport, boost your energy levels or even look you best for a special day! All of our clubs offer Personal Training sessions with fitness professionals who are at least level 3 qualified - so they will all be able to help you lose weight, tone up or increase fitness levels.

As fitness fanatics, many of our trainers specialise in specific areas such as sports therapy, injury rehabilitation, nutrition and strength & conditioning, and once you are a member you can log-on to the members area to read their profiles.

Get with the Program

Welcome to the Greenwich Fitness blog! Here, you'll discover what's new in the fitness world, including the latest views on nutrition and sports supplements as well as little known tips and secrets that will help fast track your success.

We’ve created blogs based on the most common questions we get asked in club. Upcoming topics include an overview of common workout mistakes, how to choose the right gym, the most effective workout for your goal, and how exercise can help energise you and boost your immunity. You'll also get to hear any exciting PureGym news before anyone else. Check back regularly for updates, and keep your eye out for exciting competitions.. We’d love to hear what you've got to say.- whether you agree or disagree with something we've said or want to share your own training tips and techniques, please leave your comments.
